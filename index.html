Bio

I am a UnderGraduate Student pursuing my bachelor's in Computer Science with a Specialization in Cyber Security and Forensics at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies. I am focused on Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing.

I have been a part of the Cyber Security Internship at Noida Cyber Cell . While Indulging in the internship I learned how to perform VAPT on a Web Application. During the period of 2 months I performed VAPT on Indian State Government Websites and other Web Applications. Also learned about new Tools and Techniques to perform a VAPT and the importance of reporting and technical writing of Pentest Report.

Currently, I am working on a Minor project of Network Traffic Analysis where we perform a passive sniffing and detect the Network anomaly using python. I have worked on few more projects to explore my field of interest ,that includes Steganography using LSB, SSH Botnet, Port Scanner, Unix Password Cracker.

I am constantly increasing my theoretical and practical knowledge by solving the Virtual Labs available online on the platform such as Try Hack me, Hack The Box, Portswigger Labs. Also I play CTF's occasionally and am learnig new things everyday in the cybersecurity domain.

Network Trafic Analyzer

This a CLI Tool build in python to perform a passive sniffing on the captured data packets . It is an ongoing project ,till now it analyzes the data packets and gives us the info that was a DDOS attack performed and also gives us the Geo location of the IP adresses.

Steganography and Steganalysis of an Image

SSH Botnet

This is a self build project in python using pexpect, pxssh and argparse library which could connect remotely to multiple SSH .

Unix Password Cracker
